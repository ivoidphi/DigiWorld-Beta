# DigiWorld

## Overview

You are a gaming prodigy and a 10-time world champion chosen to test the
revolutionary game **DigiWorld** created by the government.

You are transported into a digital world filled with **Mecha Beasts**,
where you fuse with them and battle challengers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Concept

-   Fuse with Mecha Beasts
-   Battle enemies and bosses
-   Progress through stages
-   Experience a fully immersive digital world

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Story Progression

### Stage 1: Alpha Village

-   Tutorial begins
-   Learn combat mechanics
-   Fight wild Mecha Beasts
-   Defeat Alpha Beast (Gekuma)

### Stage 2: Beta City

-   Fight Ace Trainer Jazz
-   Earn Challenge Ticket
-   Enter Tournament Trial
-   Face Trialmaster (Professor Alfred)

### Stage 3: Collapse of Beta City

-   Game glitch occurs
-   Hacker "Glitch" appears
-   Final boss: Woltrix
-   Save the game world

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Damage Formula

    Damage = (((2 × Level ÷ 5) + 2) × MovePower × (Attack ÷ Defense) ÷ 50 + 2)
    × STAB × TypeEffectiveness × Critical × RandomFactor
